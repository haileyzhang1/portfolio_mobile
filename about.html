About Me

Hi there! This is Jinghan Zhang and known as Hailey by my friends :) I'm currently a first year graduate student studying UX design and HCI in MSI program in University of Michigan. As a growing human-centered designer, I hope to use meaningful designs to promote individual self-growth and positive social impact. To name one, division and polarization between people, groups and communities on the internet is one of the issues that I strive to address.

Of course, when I am not brainstorming on designs, you can catch me listening to hiphop music from Post Malone and Travis Scott, playing Legend of Zelda on Nintendo switch and cooking delicious potato dishes!.
